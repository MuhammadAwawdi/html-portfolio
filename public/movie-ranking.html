The Best Movies According to Muhammad

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Shawshank Redemption

This is my favourite movie. the movie is based on a novella by Stephen King and tells the story of Andy Dufresne, a man who is wrongly convicted of murder and sent to Shawshank State Penitentiary. The story is well-crafted and engaging, with a strong narrative arc that keeps viewers hooked.

The Godfather

The movie is based on a novel by Mario Puzo and tells the story of the Corleone family, a powerful Mafia clan in New York City. The story is epic in scope and spans several decades, with complex plotlines and a large ensemble cast of characters.

The Dark Knight

The movie is a sequel to Batman Begins and continues the story of Bruce Wayne/Batman as he faces off against the Joker, a ruthless and unpredictable villain who seeks to sow chaos and anarchy in Gotham City. The story is well-crafted and suspenseful, with a strong narrative arc that keeps viewers on the edge of their seats.
